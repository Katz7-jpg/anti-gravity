- Check claims before acting
- Validate research findings
- Quality control
- Due diligence

## How to Activate

```
"Verify this claim: [statement]"
"Audit this research: [topic]"
"Check accuracy of: [information]"
```

- Source verification
- Fact-checking

- Red flags identified
